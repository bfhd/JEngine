3.2.17 ADT/ACK - swap patients (event A17)

The A17 is used when it is decided that two patients will exchange beds. The patient ID and visit data are repeated for the two patients changing places. See Section 3.5.1, "Swapping a patient" for a discussion of issues related to implementing this trigger event.
